Slow cooked Curry Chicken

This slow cooker chicken curry recipe couldn't be easier--the ingredients all go in at the same time and in just a few hours, dinner's ready! Serve over cauliflower rice sauteed with Indian spices, and garnish with fresh cilantro and a dollop of light sour cream.

ingredients

  • 3 pounds skinless, boneless chicken breast halves
  • 2 (14 ounce) cans coconut milk
  • 2 onions, diced
  • 4 tablespoons tomato paste
  • 4 tablespoons minced fresh garlic
  • 4 tablespoons minced fresh ginger
  • 1 tablespoon ground cumin
  • 1 tablespoon ground turmeric
  • i tablespoon garam masala (optional)
  • 1 tablespoon ground coriander
  • 1 tablespoon curry powder
  • 4 bay leaves
  • 1 cinnamon stick
  • salt and ground black pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Step one

    2. Add chicken, coconut milk, onions, tomato paste, garlic, ginger, cumin, turmeric, garam masala, coriander, curry powder, bay leaves, cinnamon stick, salt, and pepper to a slow cooker.

  2. Step two

    3. Cook on High until chicken is no longer pink in the center and the juices run clear, 2 to 3 hours. An instant-read thermometer inserted into the center should read at least 165 degrees F (74 degrees C). Remove and discard the bay leaves and cinnamon stick. Shred the chicken.

Cook's Notes

You can chop each chicken breast into 3 or 4 pieces to cook faster or leave whole, your preference. I prefer to chop mine because I think it makes the flavor stronger.

Nutrition facts

Per Serving: 276 calories; protein 24.4g; carbohydrates 8.7g; fat 16.8g; cholesterol 58.6mg; sodium 116.8mg.
